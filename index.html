NAPKINS





History

The term ‘napkin’ comes from the Late Middle English nappekin, from Old French nappe (tablecloth, from Latin mappa). Paper napkins
are said to originate from China in the second century AD. According to some sources, napkins started out as soft dough used by
the Spartans in Greece to clean their fingers.The Romans later introduced napkins of two different sizes: one for wiping the hands
and mouth, and one for covering the lap.

In a meal Setting:

After seated at restaurant, the next thing should be placing the napkin at lap. When placing Dinner or Bistro or Silverware napkins
in lap, the napkin should not be used to wipe face,blow nose or wipe silverware.When a meal is done, loosely fold the napkin and place
it in left or right side of your plate. Donot drop on floor or in chair.

BEVERAGE NAPKINS

Size

They are usually small in size
When fully laid out: 10" x 10" / 25cm x 25cm and
when quater folded: 5" x 5" / 13cm x 13cm

Purpose

These are small square napkins used for drinks or appetizers.
Commonly placed on the table, under the glass to soak up
any drips or condensation.Also,useful when the plate is
warm or to serve dishes to customer.

Usage

These are small square napkins and are perfect for holding a
drink or a small snack.

Material

They are usually made from paper, fabric or bamboo. Paper
napkins are not durable and thus non-resuable. But they
are biodegredable and can be composted.Bamboo napkins are
compostable and biodegradable, which makes them an eco-
friendly alternative to paper napkins. They are also soft
and durable.

DINNER NAPKINS

Size

They are large in size
When fully laid out:16-22" x 16-22"/41-56cm x 41-56cm
and When quater folded: 5" x 5" / 13cm x 13cm

Purpose

These large napkins are used for formal dining. The more formal
the dinner event is,the bigger the napkins should be.Can be used
for intricate folds and then displayed on top of a plate at the
place setting, and also are large enough to completely cover a
person’s lap

Usage

These are perfect for covering your lap and wiping your mouth
after a feast or dinner.

Material

They are usually made from fabric such as cotton or linen. Hence,
it is more economical. They can be laundered and re-used again
for usage. As they are made of fabric, they don't come pre-
folded. So, you can fold as per own desire and requirement.

SILVERWARE NAPKINS


Size

The best size to roll silverware is: 8" x 8" / 20cm x 20 cm
Generally napkin should be 3 times the height of the Knife.
The diagonal measurement of napkin from one corner to
another should be atleast 2 inches longer than the Knife.

Purpose

Silverware napkins are usually large and can be folded into a
pocket shape to hold silverware. The size of silverware
napkins is not standard, but they are usually larger than
dinner napkins and used for formal dining.

Usage

These are usually used in formal dining setting. They should be
used to fold in various pocket shapes and also can be used
to place on lap or wipe mouth after a feast or dinner.

Material

They are usually made of cotton.

LUNCHEON NAPKINS


Size

They are usually medium in size.
When fully laid out:13" x 13" / 33cm x 33cm
When quater folded:6.5" x 6.5" / 16cm x 16cm
They are little larger than Bevarage napkins and dispenser
napkins but smaller than Dinner napkins.

Purpose

These medium sized napkins are used for casual meals or
small size of food like a slice of cake. They are single-
used napkins and used mostly for like birthday parties.

Usage

These are great for wiping your mouth and hands after eating
a sandwhich, fries, burgers etc.

Material

They are again made of paper and are non-durable, non-
resuable, but compostable.

DISPENSER NAPKINS

Size

They have slightly rectangular shape
When in 3/4th fold: 12" x 13" / 30cm x 33cm
When completely folded: 6.5" x 5" / 16cm x 13cm

Purpose

They are stored in dispenser and pulled out individually.They
are widely found in diners or fast-food restaurants.They
are also found in washrooms.They are compatibe with any
dispenser.

Usage

They can be pulled out one by one or in bulk as per customer
needs.

Material

They are made of paper and can be customized as per
requirement.There are eco-friendly alternatives to paper
dispenser napkins such as cloth napkins made of organic
cotton, reusable paper towel rolls, cotton napkins, reusable
beeswax wrap, Swedish dishcloths, knitted dishcloths,pop-up
sponges, linen and cotton.

BISTRO NAPKINS

Size

They are very large in size to fit in the table anywhere.
Standard sizes comes in : 18" x 22" / 46cm x 56cm
They are square napkins slightly larger than dinner napkins.

Purpose

They came out as an alternative to other types of napkins yet
looks different, cheaper but is fancy and casual. They give
out vibe to diner as "We might look simple, but we’re
sophisticated; just feel the texture."

Usage

They are often used in restaurants and can be placed on the lap
or used to wipe the mouth.

Material

They are made of mostly linen or cloth. Often the case was that
the laundry service rent napkins to the restaurants and replace
them when they fray or become ripped; it also collects the used
cloths, washes, dries, presses, and folds them, and returns them
the next day to begin all over again.

RONDO NAPKINS


Size

They are small, round in size

When fully laid out mostly : 13" x 13"/32cm x 32cm or can say
the size ranges from 25cm x 25cm to 42 x 33cm

Purpose

Rondo napkins are often used in restaurants and used mostly for
decoration purposes in functions. They are printed on one
side and come in contents of 12-50 units.

Usage

They come as folded traingles with their rounded corners which
creates an eye-catcher on every festive table , even without
folding. Unfolded, they are a highlight on your table with flower
like appearence, both a s cake and place mats.

Material

They are made of tissue paper which is a type of paper made form
wood pulp or recycled paper pulp.
